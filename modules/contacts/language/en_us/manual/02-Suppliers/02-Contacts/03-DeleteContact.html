Deleting a Supplier Contact

NOTE: Suppliers cannot be deleted if they have a journal entry associated with them.

To delete a supplier record:

  1. Select Suppliers->Maintain Suppliers page.
  2. Select the Delete icon next to the supplier to delete. A search box has been provided to quickly locate the supplier.
  3. Confirm the deletion and you will be returned to the Maintain Suppliers page.
