Entering a Supplier Contact

Supplier contacts include any outside organization that you acquire goods or services from. Generally, they are organizations that you make payments to.

NOTE: Supplier contacts may be created at time of purchase by entering the required information and checking the Add/Update button in the Purchase/Receive, Payment or Purchase Order form. In this case, an unique contact ID must be entered and the contact will be created using the standard terms, default Purchase GL account, no User ID, Account Number, Federal ID number, or additional shipping or billing addresses.

To create a new contact:

  1. Select Suppliers->New Supplier or Select the New icon form the Suppliers->Maintain Suppliers page.
  2. Enter an unique Supplier ID. The Supplier ID (or short name) is an unique field to identify this supplier form all other suppliers. Maximum length is 20 characters.
  3. Enter remaining Primary Contact and Main Mailing (billing) Address information. Required fields are identified by a (*).
  4. Enter the default purchase account. This is typically an accounts payable general ledger account type.
  5. Enter an user id assigned to this supplier.
  6. Enter your account number with this supplier. This number can be used for purchase orders, checks to reference your account to the supplier.
  7. Enter the Tax or Federal ID number. generally used for tax purposes.
  8. If the supplier can ship from multiple locations, additional ship from addresses can be added in the Shipping Addresses form area. Unlimited shipping addresses may be assigned to a supplier but only one may be added at a time.
  9. Enter the payment terms you have with the supplier. The defaults are preloaded but may be over-ridden on a supplier by supplier basis.
  10. If the supplier has additional remit to (billing) addresses, they may be added in the Billing Addresses form area. Unlimited billing addresses may be assigned to a supplier but only one may be added at a time.
  11. Press the Save icon and the record will be saved and you will be returned to the Maintain Suppliers page.
